About Gifter

Gifter lets you track your gift ideas and purchases in one convenient place.

  • Multiple users ("givers") can maintain separate, private lists of recipients and associated gifts.
  • Create, edit and delete recipients and gifts with ease.
  • Get gift ideas from other "givers" - you won't know who the original giver or recipient was, but you can "re-give" the gift to one of your recipients.
  • Set and change your gift's "status" - is it an "idea", something you have "purchased", or something you have "given"? Gifter maintains records of when you added the gift to the system, and when you changed its status to "given."

Never lose track of your gifts and ideas again!
